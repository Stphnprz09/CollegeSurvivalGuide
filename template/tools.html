Resources and Interactive Tool

Enhance user experience on our website with interactive tools like a versatile calculator, a handy mini calendar, and an efficient daily planner for increased productivity and engagement.

Calculator

A calculator is a portable device, electronic or mechanical, designed for efficient arithmetic calculations, featuring numeric buttons, mathematical operators, and a display screen.

Calendar

The mini calendar provides users with a quick and convenient overview of dates, facilitating easy scheduling and planning.

Daily Planner

The daily planner feature on our website assists users in structuring their day, optimizing time management, and enhancing productivity through organized scheduling.

E-Notes

E-Note provides users with an interactive platform. This will help users to create and manage their own personalized notes, enhancing their experience and productivity on our website.

Tomodoro Timer

A Pomodoro timer is a productivity tool that follows the Pomodoro Technique, consisting of timed intervals, typically 25 minutes, called "Pomodoros," separated by short breaks, aiming to enhance focus, time management, and task completion efficiency.
